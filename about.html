Who we are

The Tumaini Africa Knowledge Centre (TAKC) is an African Partnership space committed to the holistic development of African societies. We focus on the alignment of African values, business solutions, Knowledge transfer and sustainable financing for high impact initiatives. At TAKC we:

  • Connect decision makers to strategic partners.
  • Identify high-net worth individuals with solutions to needs in Uganda and Africa
  • Support the installation of innovative manufacturers in Uganda.
  • Work with local entrepreneurs, mostly youth and women to join global value chains.
  • Rebrand Uganda as an investment hub in our publications and engagements.
  • Promote African heritage, resources, opportunities and markets.

TAKC will also facilitate the re-integration of Africans raised overseas by providing a space foryoung entrepreneurs as well as services to help contextualize African culture and values for people of African descent. The company’s market target is in two categories:

  1. Africans and people of African descent living both in Africa as well as outside the continent
  2. multinational investors, high net worth partners and leaders of industry with an interest in the African market.

Message from CEO...

I have accepted to serve as Chair of the Board of the Tumaini African Knowledge Center (TAKC). TAKC is an African youth-led partnership space committed to the holistic development of African societies. TAKC focuses on the alignment of African values, innovative solutions, knowledge transfer, and sustainable financing for transformational initiatives. TAKC works with high-net-worth individuals, private sector leaders, Governments, and emerging African entrepreneurs, especially youth and women, to develop solutions to fast-track the establishment of integrated value chains including access to markets in key economic sectors. As the Chairperson, I am proud to join a team of talented professionals who embody values and principles that foster innovation, collaboration, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. At TAKC, we are part of a growing community of professionals driving successful investments in Africa at a challenging time in the global marketplace. I look forward to working closely with valued clients and partners to deliver exceptional services for people and planet while also generating acceptable returns on investments. Our success will be measured by financial milestones and the positive impact in the lives of clients, employees, and communities. Thank you.

Rosa Malango

TAKC Chairperson
