Women and Young Entreprenuers

Women and young entrepreneurs partnerships aim to support and foster a culture of entrepreneurship among the women and youth in Uganda and Africa. By providing training, mentorship, and access to resources, the project seeks to empower women groups and young individuals to create sustainable businesses, drive economic growth, and contribute to the development of their communities.

We offer a blend of training, mentorship, and access to strategic resources—designed to empower visionary individuals, foster innovation, and drive inclusive economic growth.

Through this initiative, we’re cultivating a dynamic ecosystem rooted in:

  1. Capacity Building – Practical learning for real-world impact
  2. Collaboration – Connecting leaders with mentors, peers, and partner
  3. Opportunity Access – Opening doors to financing, markets, and networks
  4. Community-Driven Change – Scaling ventures that uplift and inspir

Together, we’re shaping the next generation of changemakers—one idea, one entrepreneur, one breakthrough at a time.

Our Objectives

  • To equip women and young entrepreneurs in Uganda with essential business skills and knowledge.
  • To facilitate access to financial resources and opportunities for young entrepreneurs.
  • To create Investment profiles that promotes young entrepreneurs with start-up businesses.
  • To foster an entrepreneurial ecosystem that encourages innovation, collaboration, and networking.
